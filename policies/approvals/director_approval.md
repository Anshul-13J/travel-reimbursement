# POLICY_ID: APPROVAL-002

## Category
Approvals

## Title
Director Approval

## Policy Statement

Director approval is required for:

- Alcohol expenses.
- Luxury hotels.
- International business entertainment.
- Expenses exceeding ₹50000.

## Clarification Questions

- Was Director approval obtained?
- Please provide approval documentation.

## Decision Rules

- Approval present → MANUAL_REVIEW
- Approval missing → REJECT
