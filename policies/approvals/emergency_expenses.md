# POLICY_ID: APPROVAL-003

## Category
Approvals

## Title
Emergency Expenses

## Policy Statement

Emergency expenses may be reimbursed when incurred due to:

- Medical emergencies.
- Travel disruption.
- Flight cancellation.
- Natural disasters.
- Security incidents.

Supporting evidence is mandatory.

## Clarification Questions

- What emergency occurred?
- Please provide supporting evidence.
- Was management notified?

## Decision Rules

- Evidence provided → MANUAL_REVIEW
- No evidence → REJECT
