# POLICY_ID: APPROVAL-001

## Category
Approvals

## Title
Manager Approval

## Policy Statement

Manager approval is required when:

- Meal expenses exceed policy limits.
- Hotel expenses exceed employee limits.
- Taxi expenses exceed ₹5000.

## Clarification Questions

- Was manager approval obtained?
- Please provide approval reference.

## Decision Rules

- Approval provided → APPROVE
- Missing approval → MANUAL_REVIEW
