# Company Travel Reimbursement Policy v1.0

Last Updated: January 2026

## 1. General Principles

Employees may claim reimbursement for approved business travel expenses.
All claims must:

- Be submitted within 15 days of travel completion.
- Include valid supporting receipts.
- Be directly related to business activities.
- Follow company expense limits.

Failure to comply may result in rejection or manual review.

---

## 2. Transportation

### Flights

- Domestic flights:
  - Economy class only.
  - Maximum reimbursable amount: ₹15,000 per trip.

- International flights:
  - Economy class reimbursable by default.
  - Business class requires Director approval.

---

### Train Travel

- AC Chair Car and AC 2-Tier are reimbursable.
- First Class AC requires manager approval.

---

### Local Transportation

- Taxi, cab, Uber, and Ola expenses are reimbursable.
- Maximum limit: ₹1,500 per day.
- Surge pricing above 50% requires justification.

- Personal vehicle reimbursement:
  - ₹10 per kilometer.
  - Distance proof required.

---

## 3. Accommodation

### Domestic Travel

- Maximum hotel reimbursement:
  - Tier 1 cities: ₹6,000 per night
  - Tier 2 cities: ₹4,000 per night

### International Travel

- Maximum hotel reimbursement:
  - USD 150 per night

Luxury hotels are not reimbursable without prior approval.

---

## 4. Meals

Daily meal allowance:

| Location | Limit |
|----------|--------|
| Domestic | ₹2,000/day |
| International | USD 50/day |

Alcohol expenses are not reimbursable.

---

## 5. Receipts

Receipts are mandatory for:

- Hotel stays
- Flights
- Meals above ₹500
- Taxi expenses above ₹300

Missing receipts may result in:

- Partial approval
- Manual review
- Rejection

---

## 6. Duplicate Claims

Duplicate submissions of the same expense are prohibited.

Duplicate claims will be rejected and flagged for review.

---

## 7. Submission Timeline

Claims submitted after 15 calendar days require manager approval.

Claims submitted after 30 calendar days will be rejected.

---

## 8. Manual Review Conditions

The claim must be routed for manual review if:

- Supporting documents are missing.
- Approval requirements are unclear.
- Expenses exceed policy limits by more than 20%.
- Business class travel lacks approval.
- Confidence score is below 70%.
