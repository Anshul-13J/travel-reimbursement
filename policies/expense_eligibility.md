# Expense Eligibility Rules

## Allowed Categories

- Flight
- Train
- Hotel
- Taxi
- Bus
- Metro
- Meals
- Parking
- Internet
- Visa Fees
- Conference Registration

---

## Partially Allowed Categories

### Internet

Maximum reimbursement:
- ₹500/day

---

### Parking

Maximum reimbursement:
- ₹300/day

---

### Meals

- Breakfast
- Lunch
- Dinner

Snacks above ₹200 are not reimbursable.

---

## Non-Reimbursable Expenses

The following expenses are not eligible:

- Alcohol
- Entertainment
- Movie tickets
- Shopping
- Personal sightseeing
- Spa services
- Room service luxury charges
- Traffic fines
- Personal gifts
- Family member expenses

---

## Exceptions

The following may require manual review:

- Medical emergencies
- Government restrictions
- Flight cancellations
- Natural disasters
- Executive approval exceptions
