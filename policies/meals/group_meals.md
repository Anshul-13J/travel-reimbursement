# POLICY_ID: MEAL-003

## Category
Meals

## Title
Group Meals

## Policy Statement

Business meals involving multiple attendees require supporting documentation.

Indicators of group meals include:

- More than 4 menu items.
- Total quantity greater than 4.
- Total bill amount exceeding ₹3000.
- Multiple beverages.

## Required Information

- Attendee names.
- Employee IDs.
- Client names.
- Business purpose.

## Clarification Questions

- Who attended this meal?
- Was this an internal or external meeting?
- Please provide attendee details.

## Decision Rules

- Attendee details provided → APPROVE
- Missing attendee details → MANUAL_REVIEW

## Risk Score

0.6
