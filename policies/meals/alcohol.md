# POLICY_ID: MEAL-002

## Category
Meals

## Title
Alcohol Expenses

## Policy Statement

Alcoholic beverages are not reimbursable under the standard travel policy.

Examples include:

- Beer
- Wine
- Whiskey
- Vodka
- Rum
- Gin
- Mojito
- Long Island
- Tequila
- Cocktails

## Exceptions

Alcohol expenses may be reimbursed when:

- Consumed during approved client entertainment.
- Pre-approved by Director level management.

## Clarification Questions

- Was alcohol consumed during an approved client event?
- Was prior approval obtained?
- Please provide approval reference.

## Decision Rules

- Approved client event → MANUAL_REVIEW
- No approval → REJECT
