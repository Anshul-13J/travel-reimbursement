# POLICY_ID: MEAL-001

## Category
Meals

## Title
Meal Reimbursement Limits

## Policy Statement

Employees may claim meal expenses incurred during approved business travel.

Maximum reimbursement limits are:

| Meal Type | Maximum Amount |
|-----------|---------------|
| Breakfast | ₹500 |
| Lunch | ₹1000 |
| Dinner | ₹2000 |

Any amount exceeding these limits requires manager approval.

## Exceptions

The limits may be exceeded when:

- Client meetings are involved.
- International travel is involved.
- Prior manager approval exists.

## Clarification Questions

- Which meal category does this expense belong to?
- Was manager approval obtained?
- Was this expense incurred during a client meeting?

## Decision Rules

- Within limits → APPROVE
- Above limits → MANUAL_REVIEW
- Above limits without approval → REJECT
