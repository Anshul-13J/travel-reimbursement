# Travel Expense Approval Matrix

## Domestic Travel

| Amount | Approval Required |
|---------|------------------|
| <= ₹10,000 | Auto Approval |
| ₹10,001 - ₹50,000 | Manager Approval |
| > ₹50,000 | Director Approval |

---

## International Travel

| Amount | Approval Required |
|---------|------------------|
| <= USD 500 | Manager Approval |
| USD 501 - 2000 | Director Approval |
| > USD 2000 | CFO Approval |

---

## Special Cases

The following always require manual approval:

- Business class flights
- Luxury hotels
- Emergency travel
- Claims older than 15 days
- Missing receipts
