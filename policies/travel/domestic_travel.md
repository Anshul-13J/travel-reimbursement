# POLICY_ID: TRAVEL-002

## Category
Travel

## Title
Domestic Travel

## Policy Statement

Domestic business travel expenses are reimbursable when:

- Travel is approved.
- Travel supports business operations.
- Supporting receipts are available.

Permitted:

- Flights
- Trains
- Hotels
- Taxis

## Clarification Questions

- Was this travel approved?
- Please provide travel purpose.

## Decision Rules

- Approved travel → APPROVE
- Missing approval → MANUAL_REVIEW
