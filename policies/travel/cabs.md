# POLICY_ID: TRAVEL-004

## Category
Travel

## Title
Taxi and Cab Expenses

## Policy Statement

Taxi expenses are reimbursable when incurred for business purposes.

Examples:

- Uber
- Ola
- Airport Taxi
- Corporate Cab

Expenses above ₹5000 require justification.

## Clarification Questions

- Why was taxi transport necessary?
- Was public transport available?

## Decision Rules

- Under ₹5000 → APPROVE
- Above ₹5000 → MANUAL_REVIEW
