# POLICY_ID: HOTEL-001

## Category
Hotel

## Title
Hotel Expense Limits

## Policy Statement

Hotel reimbursement limits are:

| Employee Level | Maximum Per Night |
|----------------|------------------|
| Associate | ₹5000 |
| Senior Associate | ₹8000 |
| Manager | ₹12000 |
| Director | ₹20000 |

## Exceptions

- International travel.
- Limited availability.
- Emergency travel.

## Clarification Questions

- What is the employee designation?
- Was prior approval obtained?
- Was this an emergency booking?

## Decision Rules

- Within limits → APPROVE
- Above limits → MANUAL_REVIEW
