# POLICY_ID: HOTEL-002

## Category
Hotel

## Title
Domestic Hotel Stays

## Policy Statement

Employees may claim accommodation expenses during domestic business travel.

Permitted expenses:

- Room charges
- Internet charges
- Applicable taxes

Non-reimbursable:

- Mini bar
- Spa
- Personal entertainment
- Laundry exceeding ₹1000

## Clarification Questions

- Was the stay business related?
- Were there any personal expenses?

## Decision Rules

- Business expenses → APPROVE
- Personal expenses → REJECT
