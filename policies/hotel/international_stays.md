# POLICY_ID: HOTEL-003

## Category
Hotel

## Title
International Hotel Stays

## Policy Statement

International accommodation expenses are reimbursable when associated with approved travel.

Business hotels are preferred.

Luxury hotels require approval.

Examples:

- Ritz Carlton
- Four Seasons
- JW Marriott
- Oberoi
- Taj Palace

## Clarification Questions

- Was this hotel pre-approved?
- Was a business hotel unavailable?

## Decision Rules

- Standard hotel → APPROVE
- Luxury hotel → MANUAL_REVIEW
